Your Cancer Genome Map

This is like Google Maps — but instead of streets and buildings, you're navigating cancer genes. Here's how to get around:

  • 🧬
    Click any chromosome to jump to the genes living on it. Colored marks show where cancer genes sit.
  • 🔍
    Search a gene by name (try "TP53" or "KRAS") using the search bar at the top to instantly pull up its full profile.
  • ⚙️
    Switch to Pathways view (top-right button) to see how cancer genes connect and talk to each other inside the cell.
  • 🎯
    Filter by cancer type in the left sidebar — instantly highlights only the genes relevant to that cancer.
